Privacy Policy

At salmanblogs.com, your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. We are committed to maintaining transparency and compliance with international standards such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

1. Information We Collect

We collect two main types of information: personal and non-personal. Personal information includes details you provide directly to us such as your name and email when submitting a form or subscribing to our newsletter. Non-personal information includes browser type, IP address, device type, and browsing behavior on our website.

Collecting this data helps us improve our blog content and offer you a better browsing experience. For example, if most visitors use mobile devices, we focus on optimizing mobile layouts.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to improve our website, respond to your inquiries, send newsletters if you subscribe, and analyze trends. We may also use it to personalize your experience by recommending content that matches your interests.

  • Improve blog topics and articles
  • Respond to inquiries or feedback
  • Deliver relevant ads through Google AdSense
  • Enhance overall site security and usability

3. Cookies Policy

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We use cookies to remember user preferences, analyze site traffic, and display personalized ads. For example, if you read many travel blogs, cookies may help us recommend more travel-related posts.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of our website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

4. Data Retention & Security

We keep your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. For example, if you subscribe to our newsletter, we retain your email until you unsubscribe. All personal data is stored securely using encryption and other industry-standard practices.

While we take strong measures to protect your data, no online system is 100% secure. We encourage you to take precautions such as using strong passwords and keeping your devices safe.

5. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services such as Google Analytics, Google AdSense, and EmailJS. These services may collect anonymous data such as demographics, interests, and browsing behavior. This information helps us understand our audience better and deliver content and ads that are relevant to them.

Third-party providers operate under their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

6. Children’s Privacy

Protecting children’s privacy is especially important. Our website is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we discover that a child has provided personal information, we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights regarding your data:

  • Right to access the data we hold about you
  • Right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to opt-out of data collection and advertising

To exercise these rights, please contact us through our contact page.

8. External Links Disclaimer

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party sites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before sharing any information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date. Continued use of the website after updates indicates your acceptance of the new policy.
