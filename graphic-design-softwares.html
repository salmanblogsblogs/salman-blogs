Graphic Design Software: Definition, Quick Notes & MCQs

Posted in 9 Computer Science

Full Definition

Graphic Design Software refers to computer programs used to create, edit, and design visual content such as images, logos, posters, illustrations, and digital art.

Uses: Photo editing, digital painting, logo design, illustrations, branding, posters, brochures, social media content.

Popular Examples of Graphic Design Software

  1. Adobe Photoshop – Works with pixel-based images, used for photo editing, digital art, and graphic design.
  2. Adobe Illustrator – Works with scalable graphics, commonly used for creating logos, illustrations, and designs that need to stay sharp at any size.
  3. CorelDRAW – A powerful tool for professional layouts, logos, and creative designs, known for being user-friendly.
  4. GIMP – A free, open-source program with many features similar to Photoshop, suitable for editing and design work.
  5. Canva – An online tool with ready-made templates and drag-and-drop features, useful for both beginners and professionals.

Quick Notes – Graphic Design Software

  • Graphic design software is application software used to create, edit, and design visual content like images, posters, logos, and digital artwork.
  • Uses: Photo editing, digital painting, logo design, illustrations, branding, posters, brochures, social media content.
  • It plays an important role in branding, creativity, communication, and professional design work.
  • Common uses include photo editing, digital painting, making logos, creating posters, brochures, layouts, illustrations, and social media graphics.
  • Popular examples: Photoshop, Illustrator, CorelDRAW, GIMP, Canva.

Conclusion

Graphic design software is essential for creating and editing visual content, whether for professional branding, communication, or creative expression. It provides the tools needed for photo editing, digital painting, logo design, and more. Learning these tools helps in building strong design skills useful in many fields, from business branding to digital art.

MCQs – Graphic Design Software

  1. What does graphic design software mainly help in creating?
    a) Spreadsheets
    b) Visual content
    c) Databases
    d) Programming code

    Answer: b) Visual content

  2. Which of the following is NOT a use of graphic design software?
    a) Photo editing
    b) Logo design
    c) Social media content creation
    d) File compression

    Answer: d) File compression

  3. Which software works with pixel-based images and is widely used for photo editing and digital art?
    a) CorelDRAW
    b) GIMP
    c) Adobe Photoshop
    d) Adobe Illustrator

    Answer: c) Adobe Photoshop

  4. Which software is best for creating scalable graphics like logos and illustrations?
    a) Adobe Illustrator
    b) GIMP
    c) Canva
    d) CorelDRAW

    Answer: a) Adobe Illustrator

  5. Which graphic design program is especially known for being user-friendly and powerful for professional layouts?
    a) CorelDRAW
    b) Canva
    c) Adobe Photoshop
    d) GIMP

    Answer: a) CorelDRAW

  6. Which program is free and open-source with features similar to Photoshop?
    a) Adobe Illustrator
    b) Canva
    c) CorelDRAW
    d) GIMP

    Answer: d) GIMP

  7. Which software provides an online platform with ready-made templates and drag-and-drop tools?
    a) CorelDRAW
    b) Adobe Photoshop
    c) Canva
    d) Adobe Illustrator

    Answer: c) Canva
