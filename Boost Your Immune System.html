Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

3. Does sleep really affect the immune system?

Absolutely. During deep sleep, your body produces cytokines that help fight infection and inflammation.

Yes. Vitamin D plays a vital role in immune regulation, and deficiency increases vulnerability to infections.
